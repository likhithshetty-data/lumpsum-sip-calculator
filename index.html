What is SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)?

SIP is a disciplined investment approach where you invest a fixed amount regularly (monthly/quarterly) in mutual funds. It's like a recurring deposit but with market-linked returns.

Key Benefits:

  • Rupee cost averaging reduces market timing risk
  • Power of compounding multiplies your wealth
  • Disciplined and affordable investing
  • Flexibility to start with small amounts

What is Lumpsum Investment?

Lumpsum investment means investing a large amount of money at once in mutual funds or other investment vehicles. Ideal when you have surplus funds like bonus, inheritance, or savings.

When to Choose Lumpsum:

  • You have a significant amount to invest
  • Market conditions are favorable
  • Long-term investment horizon (5+ years)
  • Maximize returns during market dips
